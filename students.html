Room Booking Rules:

  • Student ID card should be available during the entire booking.
  • Room booking request should be made minimum of 30 minutes and maximum one day in advance from the preferred time.
  • Room booking confirmation will be sent to your UOWmail address within a day - 30 mins prior to your preferred time.
  • Room booking confirmation must be presented to Security and IT office for IT assistance before using the allotted room.
  • Only one room booking request at a time is allowed for each group.
  • The following is strictly prohibited: playing loud music or any noise which may disrupt classes, unplugging of electric plugs or wires. The room should be left tidy at all times.
