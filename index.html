About



Students have been facing the problem of using their precious assignment time to
find the responsible staff and after doing so, they realised that the time slot and
vacancy of the room they desire has been taken by somebody else.
Thus, we decided to create a room booking system to save the hassle and
allow the students to book a room using the system on the go.
